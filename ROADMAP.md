# Roadmap

## 0.1 series

- panel ITS simulation and estimation;
- structural diagnostics;
- examples, tests, and bilingual documentation.

## 0.2 series

- controlled ITS with comparison groups;
- non-linear and seasonal time terms;
- visual diagnostics;
- exportable reporting tables.

## 0.3 series

- event-study workflows;
- staggered intervention support;
- robustness and placebo checks;
- additional teaching datasets.

Roadmap items are proposals. Implementation depends on validation, contributor capacity, and user demand.
